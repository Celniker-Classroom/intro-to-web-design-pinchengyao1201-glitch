About me

Personal Background

I am Pincheng Yao, born in Singapore and moved over to the US when I was 14. Currently, I am 17 years old, living in San Diego. I am a student in Canyon Crest Academy as an 11th grader. I have a younger brother that is currently in 8th grade. My parents are both born in China. My dad is from Shanghai while my mom is from Beijing. They both moved to Singapore for college and met in college. Both my parents are engineers.

Sports & Fencing

Not only am I a student but also a fencer. Fencing has been a big part of my life. I started fencing when I was 12 in Singapore and did it just for fun. But when I moved over to the US, I started competing in tournaments and found the experience enjoyable. Now, I fence in tournaments spanning from regionals to nationals. I hope to attend some international tournaments when I have the time to do so. Sports has always been a big part of my life and is what I like to do during my free time.

Future Outlook

Looking ahead, I hope to get into a university that offers a good finance/economics program and make new friends during my next 4 years. I also hope to move out of San Diego and live in other parts of the world. Many universities offer a fencing program, which is important to me as I want to continue my fencing career even after college and stay active and healthy. I aim to continue growing as a student and fencer and hope to make a positive impact on the people close to me.
